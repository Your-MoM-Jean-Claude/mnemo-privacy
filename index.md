
Privacy Policy for Mnemo
Last updated: May 2026

Mnemo does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

All data (word lists, statistics, settings) is stored exclusively
on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults and local
app storage). Nothing is sent to any server.

The app requests permission to send local notifications solely to
deliver your optional daily study reminder. No notification data
is shared with third parties.

We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party
tracking.
